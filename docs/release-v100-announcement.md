Hey {firstName},

We cut 60% of the dashboard clutter. The new one answers the three
questions you actually ask in the morning — what needs attention, what's
scheduled, what money came in — and nothing else.

- **Nudge customers in one tap.** No more typing "just checking in" by
  hand. Save your wording once in Settings → Messages, and every follow-up

  they get a text from you — not a cryptic Stripe email.
- **Amendments merged into the main quote.** Customers see the original
  and the changes as one document, with additions highlighted.

Prefer the old dashboard? Classic view is one click away in your sidebar
for the next 30 days.

[**Open your dashboard →**](https://punchlist.ca/dashboard)
